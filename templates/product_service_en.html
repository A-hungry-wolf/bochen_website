Data labeling

The company has close cooperation with many top laboratories in academia or top companies in industry, and maintains in-depth exchanges with the most senior scholars, professors and research institutes in the field of neuroscience and artificial intelligence. Detailed and complete 3d reconstruction of different brain regions (e.g., cerebellum, visual cortex, prefrontal cortex, etc.) in different species (e.g., mice, rats, humans, etc.) has been performed at the nanoscale to obtain a map of neural connections (also known as [connectome]) of such brain tissue samples.
